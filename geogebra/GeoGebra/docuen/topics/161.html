Additional keyboard commands:

·        Alt-Shift (Mac OS: Ctrl-Shift): Upper-case Greek letters

·        Spreadsheet View: Ctrl-Alt-C copies values (not the formulae)

 

Note: The degree symbol ° (Alt-O, Mac OS: Ctrl-O) and the symbol π for pi (Alt-P, Mac OS: Ctrl-P) can also be used in the slider dialog window for interval (min, max) and the increment.
